How Scheme Linking Works

  1. During registration, you declare applicable schemes and upload supporting documents
  2. Our verification engine cross-checks with government databases (Aadhaar, SECC, employer records)
  3. Eligible schemes are automatically linked to your Smart Card
  4. At point of care, hospital's system detects linked scheme and applies benefits automatically
  5. No separate scheme card or document needed — your Smart Card is the single access point
