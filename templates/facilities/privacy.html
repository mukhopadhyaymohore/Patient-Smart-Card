Privacy

Consent & Privacy Control

You own your health data. The Patient Smart Card gives you granular control over who can see what — with a complete audit trail of every access, every time.

Key Features
🎛️

Granular Permissions

Control access per record type, per provider, and per time window. Share only what's needed for each visit.

👁️

Access Audit Trail

See exactly who accessed your records, when, from which facility, and what they viewed — in real time.

🚫

Instant Revocation

Revoke any provider's access to your records instantly from the app — takes effect in under 30 seconds.

🕐

Time-Limited Access

Grant access for a specific duration — e.g. only during this admission, or only for the next 7 days.

🔐

Sensitive Record Vaulting

Mental health, reproductive health, and HIV-related records can be vaulted — requiring extra consent to access.

📤

Data Portability

Export your complete health data in standard formats (FHIR, PDF) at any time. Your data, your right.

Consent Tiers
Emergency

Emergency Access — No Consent Required

First responders can read critical data (blood group, allergies, emergency contacts) without PIN in life-threatening situations.

Treating

Treating Doctor — Card Tap Consent

Doctors you present your card to can access your full EHR during the active consultation. Access ends after discharge.

Research

Research / Analytics — Explicit Opt-In Only

Anonymised data may be used for health research only with your explicit, separate opt-in consent. Opt out anytime.

Third Party

Third Parties — Blocked by Default

Insurance companies, employers, and advertisers cannot access your health data. Sharing requires your explicit approval each time.

Legal Compliance

DPDP Act 2023

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act — full compliance with data minimisation, purpose limitation, and consent requirements.

HIPAA Standards

Meets HIPAA administrative, physical, and technical safeguard standards for health data protection.

NHA ABDM Guidelines

Compliant with Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission's Health Data Management Policy and ABHA standards.

ISO 27001

Information security management certified. Regular third-party audits ensure ongoing compliance and data safety.
