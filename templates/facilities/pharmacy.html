How E-Prescription Works
🩺

Doctor Consults

Doctor accesses your records via Smart Card and writes a digital prescription in the system.

⚡

Instant Delivery

Prescription is sent electronically to your preferred partner pharmacy or the nearest one.

🔍

Allergy Check

System automatically cross-checks against your allergy and medication history. Alerts raised if conflict found.

💊

Dispensed & Recorded

Pharmacy dispenses medicines. Transaction is logged to your EHR automatically — no paper bills needed.

🔔

Refill Reminders

System tracks your dosage schedule and sends reminders when medications are running low.
