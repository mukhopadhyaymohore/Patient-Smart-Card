Key Features
🔓

No PIN Required

Emergency data is accessible without biometrics or PIN — readable by any NFC-enabled device or QR scanner.

🏥

ER Protocol Integration

Empanelled hospitals' emergency departments are trained to scan Smart Cards on arrival for all unresponsive patients.

📞

Auto Emergency Alert

When your card is scanned in emergency mode, your registered emergency contacts receive an automatic SMS alert.

💉

Allergy Warnings

Drug allergies are prominently flagged to prevent administration of contraindicated medications during emergencies.

🩸

Blood Bank Link

Card triggers automated blood bank query at the nearest facility for your blood group — saving critical time.

📋

Insurance Pre-Auth

Emergency admissions trigger automatic insurance pre-authorisation — no delays due to paperwork during crisis.

What Happens When Your Card Is Scanned in an Emergency

  1. First responder or ER staff taps card on NFC reader or scans QR
  2. Critical data displays instantly — blood group, allergies, conditions, contacts
  3. Emergency contact receives automatic SMS with hospital name and location
  4. Insurance pre-auth is triggered automatically for cashless emergency admission
  5. Full EHR becomes available once treating doctor authenticates with their credentials
