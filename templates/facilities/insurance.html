Cashless Insurance Claims

Zero paperwork, zero waiting. Your Smart Card handles pre-authorisation and claim settlement automatically — whether for OPD, surgery, or emergency admission.

Key Features

Instant Pre-Authorisation

Pre-auth requests are sent to your insurer automatically when you're admitted — approval in minutes, not hours.

📋

Zero Paperwork

No claim forms to fill. Hospital submits all required documents digitally on your behalf.

🔔

Real-Time Updates

SMS and app notifications at every stage — pre-auth sent, approved, claim filed, settlement done.

🏥

5,000+ Network Hospitals

Cashless treatment at all empanelled hospitals. No upfront payment required at the time of admission.

💰

Auto Reimbursement

📊

Claims Dashboard

How Cashless Claims Work
🏥

Hospital Admission

You present your Smart Card at the empanelled hospital. Staff scans it to pull your insurance details.

📤

Pre-Auth Sent

Hospital sends pre-authorisation request to your TPA/insurer with your diagnosis and estimated cost.

✅

Approval Received

Insurer approves the claim within minutes. You receive an SMS confirmation. Treatment proceeds cashlessly.

💊

Treatment Delivered

You receive full treatment. Hospital bills are settled directly between the hospital and insurer.

🤝

Final Settlement

Any co-pay or deductible amount is clearly communicated. You only pay what's not covered — nothing more.
