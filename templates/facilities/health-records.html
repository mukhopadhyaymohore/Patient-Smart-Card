Key Features
📲

Auto-Sync from Labs

Lab results are pushed directly to your EHR the moment they are ready — no manual uploads needed.

🔍

Instant Doctor Access

Authorised doctors can pull your full history with a single card tap — no need to carry files or CDs.

📊

Trend Analysis

View how your health metrics — HbA1c, BP, cholesterol — have changed over months and years.

🔒

Granular Access Control

Choose exactly which records to share with which doctor. Revoke access anytime from the app.

☁️

Lifetime Cloud Storage

Records are stored permanently on secure government-grade cloud — never lost even if your card is replaced.

📥

Download & Share

Export any record as a PDF and share with doctors, insurance providers, or family members securely.

How Records Are Added
🏥

Hospital Visit

Doctor's consultation notes, diagnosis, and prescriptions are auto-saved to your EHR after every visit.

🧪

Lab Tests

Partner lab results are synced automatically. For non-partner labs, upload via the app or portal.

💊

Pharmacy

Every dispensed medication is logged — building a complete, accurate medication history over time.

🩺

Telemedicine

Online consultation notes and e-prescriptions are added to your EHR just like in-person visits.

📱

Self-Upload

Upload documents from older visits, physical reports, or records from non-empanelled hospitals manually.
